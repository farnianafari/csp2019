FARNIA NAFARIFARD


Hi I'm Farnia!! I was born and spent most memorable part of my life in Tehran, Iran where I lived for 14 years and finished middle school at NODET: Farzanegan 5.


In 2015, I started high school in the United States. Right now, I'm a senior at Rancho Bernardo High School. Living in San Diego in the past four years has been a great experience for me and my family.


I enjoy reading books, watching movies, and of course being artistic!


Due to my love for art and architetcure, I became a part of Art and Mural Club at RBHS. This year we're focusing on a large mosaic mural project that soon will be on the school wall across the library.
